One Happy Moment:

2015

seeking joy...

"One Happy Moment" is a project by Una Kravets as a practice in gratitude and positivity. Every day of 2015, she has written down one happy moment, no matter how small, to remember the day by. This site was created by using Kimono to turn her goals repo into an API, and Express on Heroku to serve it. The typeface used is Roboto. Her code is open, as always.
